Welcome to optiMize.

optiMize is a community for people who are passionate about social change and want to take action.

Our Social Innovation Challenge provides support, resources and $100,000 for student teams to turn ideas into impact.
The community is led by a core team of students and supported by a dedicated group of mentors and partners.
We’re working with the University of Michigan to make real-world impact an integral part of the college experience.

Anyone is welcome, and no experience is necessary. We hope you’ll join us.
